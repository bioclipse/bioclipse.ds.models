Data source

Data originates from the PubChem BioAssay 2796, named Luminescence-based cell-based high throughput confirmation assay for activators of the Aryl Hydrocarbon Receptor. The data was filtered for unknown CDK atom types, and contained after this filtering 15951 compounds where 7971 were annotated as Active, and 7980 compounds as Inactive.
