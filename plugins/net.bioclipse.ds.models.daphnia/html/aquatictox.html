Aquatic Toxicity

Aquatic toxicology is the study of the effects of manufactured chemicals on aquatic organisms.

Daphnid assay

Daphnia are small, planktonic crustaceans widely used in aquatic toxicity testing. A list of structural alerts (SMARTS) consiting of 18 manually created SMARTS from the publication below was kindly supported by Emma Schymanski.
