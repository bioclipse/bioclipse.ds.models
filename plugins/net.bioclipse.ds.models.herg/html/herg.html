hERG

Human Ether-a-go-go-Related Gene (hERG) encodes the the alpha subunit of a potassium ion channel, best known for its contribution to the electrical activity of the heart that coordinates the heart's beating. If inhibited or compromised, such as by a drug, it can result in a potentially fatal disorder called long QT syndrome. hERG inhibition is hence an important antitarget that must be avoided during drug development.
